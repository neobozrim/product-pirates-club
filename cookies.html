Introduction

This Cookies Policy explains how Product Pirates Club ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on the website https://productpirates.club/ ("the Website"). By accessing or using our Website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. Cookies allow websites to recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or past actions.

How We Use Cookies

At Product Pirates Club, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze our website performance, and understand how users interact with the website to make improvements.

1. Analytics and Performance Cookies

We use performance cookies to understand how visitors engage with our Website. These cookies help us track page views, session duration, and user interactions such as clicks on buttons and links. The analytics tools we use include:

  • Google Universal Analytics: We use cookies provided by Google Analytics to gather insights about how visitors use our Website, including page views and time spent on the site. These cookies help us understand user behavior and improve website functionality.
  • Amplitude: We use Amplitude to track page views, session length, and interactions with elements like buttons or links. This helps us analyze user engagement and improve our website content and design.
  • Hotjar: We use Hotjar to record user sessions and generate heatmaps to understand how users interact with our Website. In the future, we may also use Hotjar to collect feedback through polls or surveys.

2. Third-Party Cookies

Our Website uses third-party cookies set by the following services:

  • Google Universal Analytics
  • Amplitude
  • Hotjar

These cookies are only used for performance analytics and are not shared with any third-party advertisers.

3. Necessary Cookies

We do not use any cookies that are essential for the basic functionality of the Website. Your interaction with our Website remains unaffected if cookies are disabled.

Cookie Consent and Management

We display a cookie banner when you first visit our Website to inform you about our use of cookies. You have the option to accept or decline cookies.

How to Control Cookies

You have the right to control and manage cookies in the following ways:

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to manage your cookie preferences through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies.
  • Third-Party Tools: You can opt out of data collection through cookies by using tools provided by third parties like Google Analytics Opt-out Tool and Amplitude Privacy Settings.
