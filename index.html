Prototyping for Product People

Quick Prototyping using ML/AI tools and applying ML/AI algorithms

Every product person should be able to put together a quick proof of concept independently from their tech team.

During the workshop you are going to build a few prototypes using different ML/AI tools. Part of your prototypes are going to use powerful ML/AI algorithms.

While prototyping, we are also cover some high level theory, so that you have a foundational understanding of key ML algorithms, how they work and what type of tasks they are best suited for.

Quick Prototyping using ML/AI tools and applying ML/AI algorithms

It takes weeks before you get initial results from a data analysis that you have requested only to find out that it is not exactly what you needed. Discovery Hands-on

Quick Prototyping using ML/AI tools and applying ML/AI algorithms

Discovery is about getting an intimate understanding of your customers’ context and how your product fits in that. Once you have that context you gain the power to interpret it and brainstorm solutions.

Discovery is not something you should ask your manager for permision to do. Yet this is the case in many organisations.

This workshop has two goals: gain a very strong hands-on experience in doing stellar discovery work. Yup, we are going to do actual discovery work together!
